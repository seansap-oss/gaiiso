About Us

Rooted in Imphal. Made with care.

GAAISO brings together handcrafted jewellery, curated hampers and naturally styled gifts for people who value thoughtful details.

Our story

GAAISO is imagined as a quiet boutique space where craft, gifting and personal expression meet. The brand celebrates handmade feeling, soft detail and thoughtful presentation.

From jewellery pieces to curated hampers, every product is presented with warmth and care so visitors can discover the collection like turning pages in a handmade photo album.

The craft feeling

The website keeps the design minimal so the photographs, materials and products stay at the centre. Torn-paper edges, soft shadows and warm colours give the site a handmade identity without making it messy.

Future upgrades can include UPI payments, order tracking, customer messages and more product collections.
